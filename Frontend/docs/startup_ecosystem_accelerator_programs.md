## Accelerator Programs

### GrowX Acceleration Program

The CoE runs GrowX, a comprehensive acceleration program designed to accelerate deep-tech startups. The program provides various benefits to participating startups:

#### Program Benefits
- **Computing Infrastructure:** Access to high-performance computing resources
- **Co-Working Space:** Dedicated workspace facilities
- **AI Experience Zone:** Showcase area for demonstrating AI solutions
- **GTM Support:** Go-to-market strategy and execution support
- **Funding Support:** Access to funding opportunities and investor networks
- **Mentorship:** Expert guidance from industry leaders
- **Solution Showcase Opportunities:** Platform to demonstrate solutions to potential clients

#### Program Focus Areas
GrowX focuses on accelerating startups in key technological domains while preparing them for market success and scaling.

## Educational and Mentorship Initiatives

### Innovation Masterclasses
The CoE conducts specialized Innovation Masterclasses for startups covering essential business and technical topics:

- **Intellectual Property Rights:** Understanding IP protection and commercialization
- **Investment Funding:** Fundraising strategies and investor relations
- **Cloud Computing:** Leveraging cloud technologies for scalable solutions
- **Design Validation:** Product development and validation methodologies

### Mentorship Program
The CoE maintains a panel of experienced mentors who provide guidance across critical startup growth areas:

- **Product-Market Fit:** Achieving market validation and customer alignment
- **Design Validation:** Technical and user experience validation
- **Fundraising:** Capital raising strategies and valuation optimization
- **Market Reach:** Market penetration and expansion strategies

Mentors are accessible through the CoE website, where startups can request sessions based on their specific needs.

## Startup Support Framework

### Eligibility Criteria for AI Innovation Challenge
Deep-tech startups participating in CoE programs must meet specific criteria:

- **Annual Turnover:** Not exceeding Rs. 25 crore
- **Company Age:** Not exceeding 10 years from date of incorporation
- **Team Size:** Not more than 100 employees

### Program Benefits for Winners
- **Proof of Concept Development:** Paid PoC development support
- **CoE Membership:** 1-year membership in AI CoE Startup Accelerator Program
- **Commercial Connections:** CoE facilitates connections with use case owners (commercial terms determined by owners)

### Typical Visitor Questions (FAQ View)

- **“How many startups are currently incubated at the AI CoE?”**  
  There are **18 deep-tech startups** formally incubated at the Nasscom AI Center of Excellence in GIFT City. The list above always reflects the current cohort.

- **“How many startups has the CoE supported overall?”**  
  Beyond the 18 incubated startups, the CoE has **supported 500+ startups** through mentoring, pilots, accelerators, and ecosystem programs.

- **“Which sectors see the highest startup activity?”**  
  Healthcare, agriculture & food tech, enterprise AI, and manufacturing/industrial automation together account for **12+ startups**, with additional activity in legal tech, aviation, and wellness.

- **“What’s the typical journey of a startup at the CoE?”**  
  Startups usually begin with **diagnostics and mentoring**, then move into **PoC or pilot engagements** with enterprises or government departments, and finally progress to **scaled deployments** or follow-on funding supported by GrowX and partner networks.

- **“How does GrowX relate to the incubated startups?”**  
  GrowX is the **acceleration layer** on top of incubation—it focuses on market access, GTM, and scale-up for high-potential startups, including those incubated at the AI CoE as well as selected external startups.
