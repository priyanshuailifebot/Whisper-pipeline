# Government AI Initiatives & Success Stories

## AI Center of Excellence (CoE) Overview

### Establishment and Mission
The AI Center of Excellence (CoE) was established through a partnership between Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), state governments, and Nasscom. Inaugurated in July 2016 as part of the Digital India Initiative, the CoE focuses on democratizing innovation in emerging technologies including Internet of Things (IoT) and Artificial Intelligence (AI).

The CoE serves as an intelligent information hub for training and visitor engagement at Nasscom AI Centre of Excellence, enabling deep tech startup ecosystem growth with emphasis on IP creation and market development.

### Strategic Locations
The CoE operates from four strategic locations:
- Bangalore
- Gurugram
- Gandhinagar
- Vizag

### Key Achievements (First Phase)
- Labs operating at full occupancy
- Robust industry connections with 1000+ enterprises including SMEs
- Showcase for international visitors on India's technological innovations
- Pioneering thought leadership with 300+ events & workshops
- Societal impact through initiatives like "Jan Care" demonstrating digital technologies for public good

### Gujarat AI CoE Establishment
Following five years of operation (2019-2024), Nasscom signed an MoU with Gujarat Informatics Limited and Department of Science & Technology to establish AI Center of Excellence at GIFT City, Gandhinagar. Inaugurated by Hon'ble Chief Minister of Gujarat on 27th January 2025.

The AI CoE focuses on:
- Sector-specific AI use case development
- Pilot projects
- Training facilitation for government officials on AI
- Secure data exchange
- Adoption of AI models across government departments and private sectors

## Success Stories

### AI-Powered Grain Quality Assessment at APMCs

**Problem Statement:** Grain quality evaluation relied on manual and subjective inspection, leading to inconsistent results, lack of transparency, and unfair pricing for farmers at local marketplaces.

**Solution:** AI-based grain quality assessment system evaluating over ten physical parameters within 30 seconds using computer vision.

**Impact:**
- Deployed at APMCs in Kheda and Anand, Gujarat
- Enabled scientific, data-driven decision-making at grassroots level
- Empowered 1,000+ farmers
- Facilitated grain trade worth ₹700+ crore
- Reduced losses from rejections and ensured fair pricing

**Reference:** https://youtu.be/_R_zBiva1a8?si=z4YhCnsNVKYP5Rpv

### Quick Facts & Visitor FAQs

- **“How many farmers were directly impacted?”**  
  The APMC grain quality deployment has **empowered 1,000+ farmers**, improving price transparency and reducing disputes at mandis.

- **“What is the economic value of grain traded using this system?”**  
  The solution has already facilitated **grain trade worth ₹700+ crore**, demonstrating that AI can meaningfully scale to high‑value government marketplaces.

- **“Where is this deployed today?”**  
  The current deployment is at **APMCs in Kheda and Anand districts of Gujarat**, with the architecture designed so it can be replicated in other markets and states.

- **“What does the government gain from this?”**  
  Government agencies gain **auditable, data‑backed evidence** of grain quality, faster dispute resolution, and a clear benchmark for future agricultural digitalization projects.

## Policy Implementation and Incentives

### Gujarat IT/ITeS Policy (2022-27) - Deep Tech Focus

#### Special Incentives for ICT & Deep Tech Startups
- **R&D, Prototype Creation and Product Development Support:** One-time support of 25% up to INR 25 Lakhs, reimbursed upon first sales invoice
- **Patent Assistance:** 75% of patent costs (up to INR 5 lakhs for domestic, INR 10 lakhs for international patents)
- **Quality Certification Support:** 50% subvention for up to three certifications, maximum INR 5 Lakhs per certificate
- **Shared Infrastructure Support:** 35% of bandwidth and cloud costs for 6 months (up to INR 10 Lakhs for non-incubated, INR 7.5 Lakhs for incubated startups)
- **Lease Rental Support:** Up to INR 25/sq.ft. or actual rental, whichever lower, for 5 years
- **Special Incentives:** Additional support as per section 2.7.2

#### Deep Tech Specific Benefits
- One-third relaxation in eligibility criteria
- Extended depreciation period (5 years) for deep tech hardware
- Technical Committee validation for deep tech entities

#### Infrastructure Support
- **Deep Tech Centre of Excellence:** CAPEX and OPEX support with GPU compute capacity for R&D, training, and IP commercialization
- **Data Centers:** Tier III+ facilities with 4,000+ sq.ft. or 900 KW power consumption or 2,00,000 processor cores
- **Global In-house Centers (GIC/GCC):** Minimum INR 25 Cr GFCI with 75% deep tech operations

## National Context - IndiaAI Mission

### IndiaAI Impact Summit 2026
First global AI summit in Global South, hosted in New Delhi (February 19-20, 2026), anchored on three foundational 'Sutras' and seven interconnected 'Chakras' for holistic societal transformation.

### Seven Pillar Mission
1. **IndiaAI Innovation Centre:** Indigenous Large Multimodal Models and domain-specific foundational models
2. **IndiaAI Application Development Initiative:** Scalable socio-economic AI solutions
3. **AIKosh Platform:** Unified hub for datasets, models, AI sandbox, and resources
4. **IndiaAI Compute Capacity:** 10,000+ GPU ecosystem through public-private partnerships
5. **IndiaAI Startup Financing:** Streamlined funding access for deep-tech AI startups
6. **IndiaAI FutureSkills:** AI education expansion and Data/AI Labs in Tier 2/3 cities
7. **Safe & Trusted AI:** Responsible AI governance with tools, checklists, and frameworks

### Global IndiaAI Summit 2024
Focused on advancing responsible AI development, deployment, and adoption across seven pillars, organized in New Delhi.
